Overview

The ARC Backend Theme is a customizable theme for the Odoo platform, designed to enhance the user experience and improve productivity. Developed by Ksolves India Ltd. , the theme offers modern and sleek designs, customizable home menus, and several other useful features.

Role

UI UX Designer

Duration

12 weeks (October 2023 - March 2024)

Tools

Figma, Lottie files

The Problem

The ARC Backend Theme was initially found to be non-intuitive and restrictive by its users, leading to decreased productivity and an increased volume of support requests. Main challenges included complex navigation, poor visual hierarchy, and limited customization options, which failed to meet the diverse needs of the various user types ranging from business users to IT managers.

Solution

A comprehensive redesign focusing on user-centric improvements was initiated. The solution aimed to enhance navigation, introduce a customizable interface that users could tailor to their preferences and operational needs, and integrate advanced tools for error detection and user activity monitoring to increase efficiency and ease of use.

Reflections and Learnings

  • Reflective Insights: The redesign process highlighted the critical importance of deeply understanding user needs and the value of iterative testing and feedback in UX design.
  • Project Impact: This project demonstrated how thoughtful design could significantly enhance user satisfaction and operational efficiency.
  • Future Applications: The learnings from this project emphasized the ongoing need for adaptability and responsiveness to user feedback to maintain relevance and effectiveness of the interface.

Conclusion

The strategic redesign of the ARC Backend Theme has not only improved usability but also positioned the product as a competitive leader in customizable ERP solutions. This case study underscores the vital role of user-centered design and iterative development in successful UX outcomes.
